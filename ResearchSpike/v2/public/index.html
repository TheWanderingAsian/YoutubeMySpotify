YoutubeMySpotify

How to use:

  1. Open Spotify
  2. Play (good) music on Spotify
  3. Watch the player update with the music video from Youtube

But First!

You need to log in to Spotify here so we know who's music to listen to!

Log in with Spotify



By the way...

The music video length will take priority over the song length on Spotify. To do this, we will pause Spotify when it nears the end of a song, preventing your music video being cut to the length of the song.
